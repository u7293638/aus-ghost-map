ST. THOMAS'S. NOETH SYDNEY. At St. Thomas's, North Syiney, yesterday, the Vicar (the Eev. S. H. Childe) presented thirty eight candidates (twenty-five young women and thirteen young men), to receive the rite Ifotn the hands of the Primate. Dr. Smith also preached at the evening service, when Barnby's anthem, ' Gome, Holy Ghost,' was rendered. Mr. Joseph Maseey also gave an organ recital.
