Mr. Henniker Heaton. (FROM OUR CORRESPONDENT.) LONDON, March 13. Robert Dennis bas sued Mr. Henniker Heaton, MP. for Canterbury, for the recovery of £500, alleged to be due to plaintiff for writing speeches, articles in the National Review, and letters to the Times , against the Postmaster General (Right Hon, H. C. Raikes) from 1885 to 1888, also his statement read before the Colonial Conference, and for acting as Henniker Heaton's 'ghost.' The action has been settled by pay ment of £400, and £200 costs, to plaintiff.
