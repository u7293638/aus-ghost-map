AMUSEMENTS. The Cyclorama of the Battle of Waterloo still. continues to attract large attendances daily, the illustrative lecture by Mr. Lookley and the"*, descriptive music by the orchestra, under the direction of Mr. Dennis Keenan, adding greatly to the pleasing nature of the entertainment. The ghost of Napoleon and the enchanted fountain of the laughing waters are nightly greeted with enthusiastic applause, and altogether the exhibition is one that should be seen by everybody.
