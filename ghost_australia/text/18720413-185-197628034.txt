THEATRE ROYAL. At the Theatre Royal on Friday evening Mrs Glad stane took her benefit as Rosalind in “As You Like It," and as Katherine in “Katherine and Petruchio.” The house was full and the applause enthusiastic. It need not be said, that' Mrs Gladstane’a appreciation of the characters was in the highest degree ' intelligent and forcible. This evening Mrs Gladstone will appear as Nancy Sikes in the drama “ Oliver Twist,” and the evening’s entertainment will conclude with “ A Ghost in Spite of Himself.” There should be a capital Saturday night’s house.
