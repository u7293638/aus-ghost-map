SPORTING NEWS. FOOTBALL. The second twenties of the Holy Ghost College and the Ballarat College played a capital game on Tuesday. There was almost as much excitement over the game as if it was over the contest for premiership The Holy Ghost College won with a lot to spare. The B.C. played a masterly game The shots for goal on both sides were in most instances wide of the mark. The result was—four goals 12 behinds to seven behinds.
