HIS RESOLVE. HE paused outside his own front door, And with a deep drawn sigh, The keyhole, with a pencil case, In vain to fit did try. But to his "beer-befuddled" brain The door went round and round ; And forty keyholes, all the same, * Did dance upon the ground. In wild despair which one to take- Supported by a post— He vainly followed with his hand The keyhole's active ghost. Until at last, whilst wonder strange His beery features bore, He said, " I guess there's something up (hic)- I'll try (Bh) another door. < THE DARK AGES.—Ladies, when past twenty-six.
