AMUSEMENTS. Hudson's Surprise Party performed at the Bijou Theatre last night to a very large and enthusiastic audience, encores following almost every item on the programme. Mr. James Watt's c-lever female impersonation and extraordinary legmania dance excited much enthusiasm, and Mr. Hudson's ghost illusion created much laughter. The present programme will be repeated for the last time this evening, another change being promised for to-morrow. The .Surprise Party terminate their present successful season on Wednesday evening of next week. Then they will visit Gawler, Petersburg, and'Broken Hill.
