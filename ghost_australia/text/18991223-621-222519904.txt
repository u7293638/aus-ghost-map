DISPIRITING. .. Mrs Hardup: "Do you think there is a chance of our having any spirit this Christmas', John?" Mr Hardup (sighingly): "Not the ghost of a chance!" DEAR OLD CUSTOMS. "What with hills, Christmas boxes, charities, presents, general hospitality, and theatre tic kets," said the housekeeper grumpily, "they may well talk about the dear old customs. 'Christmas comes but once a year,"'eh? 'Well, if it came twice, it 'ud have to come on a veeeterian principle, and no tips the second time."
