NEW SOUTH WALES. A MISSING ISLAND TRADER. SYDNEY, FRIDAY. The mysterious disappearance of the inter island trader, The Sea Ghost, with two British subjects on board, is reported from the Solomon Group. The Sea Ghost went up the. Bouganville Straits trading several months ago, and up to the time the Chittoor sailed for Sydney nothing had been heard of either vessel or her crew. A trader has re ported having picked up wreckage, which was supposed to be from The Sea Ghost. He believed that the crew had been mur dered by the natives, the vessel being looted and then cast ashore to hide all traces ot the murder.
