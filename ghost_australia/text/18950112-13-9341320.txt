A HAUNTED HOUSE. Oiv ing to a rumour having been circulated that a house in Bank street west, South Melbourne, wife haunted, the residents in the neighbourhood have been annoyed for some time past by the hundreds of persons who have congregated in the locality. Complaints were made to the local police, and constables were sent down to move the crowd on. At the local court yesterday) morning a young man named Alfred Needham was charged with loitering on the footway. The defendant admitted that he went to see the ghost, but stated that he walked away when told to do to by the police. He was fined 10s. or three days' imprisonment.
