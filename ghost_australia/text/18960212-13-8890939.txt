A STUPID JOKE. ROCHESTER, TUESDAY. Some consternation has been caused during the past week in the west portion of the town by the appearance at late hours of the night of some person playing "ghost" Numbers of women and children have been greatly frightened by unexpected visits, and on Sunday night the sudden fright caused by the appearance had the effect of placing a very old and respected lady in a very critical condition. Numerous parties were out last night on the watch, but the "ghost" did not put in an appearance. The police are also making a searching inquiry into the matter, and have elicited evidenee which will no doubt shortly lead to the arrest of the individual who is practising these silly tricks
