Tasmanian International Exhibition. m. At the children's afternoon matinee and in the evening large audiences were present at Mr. Ernest Hosking's society entertainment. His Excellency the Governor and Lady Gormanston attended the final performance, and were amongst those who most appreciated the cleverly-executed bill of fare. Miss Ada Fitzroy introduced her silhouettes in the evening. The spiritualistic seance was again a success, the ghosts walking: and talk- ing, and generally mystifying everybody. Intercolonial exhibitors at Wednesday's fruit show have already been inspecting the space allotted them. To-night a new baritone In Mr. T. E.
