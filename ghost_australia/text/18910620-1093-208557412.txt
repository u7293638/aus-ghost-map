MANOORA COURSING. FOURTH DAT. [By Telegraph.] Manoora, June 19; The Martindale meeting of the Manoora Coursing Club was concluded to-day, when the Drawn Stakes were run. One hundred specta tors followed, and it was the best day's cours ing of_ the meeting, every hare being strong and without exception gruellers. They were very numerous, some places being infested with game. Twenty-three courses were run off by 2 o'clock, and the final after an interval. Waterside, Masher, Chemist, Belle, O.Y.G., and Hermit were drawn, starting from the woolshed at half-past 9. The results were:-Z Kintore beat Marley's Ghost. Mark Tapley beat Gertie.
