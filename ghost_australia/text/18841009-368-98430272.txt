To Nick's Sister. A story is told of a Scotchwoman who tried to wean her husband from the public.house by employing her brother to act the part of a ghost and frighten John on his way home. "Wha are you?" said the guidman, as the apparition rose before him from behind abush. " I am Auld Nick," was the reply. "Come awa', man," said John,nothing daunted; "gie's a shake o' your hand-I am married tae a sister o' yours."
