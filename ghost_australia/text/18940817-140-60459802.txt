At dinner on that first day of our departure when. the ship was at sea and I was stumping the deck in charge, I observed, in glancing through the skylight, that the captain had put Miss Le Grand upon the right of his chair at the head of the table, a little before the fluted and emblazoned shaft of the mizzenmast.
