THE CYCLOBAMA AT POPULAR DAY PRICES. It is announced that visitors to the Cyclo rama of Waterloo during the day time wiil be admitted at the popular scale of Is. for adults and 6d. for children between the hours of 10 a.m. and 5 p.m. This decision has been arrived at from the fact that more value is now given at the Cyclorama in the evening than during the day, and to compensate for this the departure has been made by the manage ment of the popular resort. The evening programme is made additionally attractive by 'Napoleon's Ghost,' the 'Enchanted Foun tains,' and other effects, and the usual scale of 2a. and Is. will be maintained.
