THE V.A.T.C. MEETING. [By Telegraph.] Melbourne, June 30. There was a large amount of betting to-day.over the Ladies' Bracelet, to be IUU for at Caulfield tomorrow. There was a great rush on Verdure for the Bracelet, and all the available money was taken to 7 to 1. The Ghost receded to 10 to 1, at which price Larpent is firm. Squireen, King's Head, and King Charles are backed at 100 to 6. For V.R.C. Derby Segenhoe is firm at 4 to 1; Navigator is easier, 7 to 1 being offeied.
