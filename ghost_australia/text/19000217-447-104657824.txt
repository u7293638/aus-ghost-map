Spies. We never thought the "spy" system, which brought English rule into so much disfavour in Ireland, would take root in the free air of Australia. However, there is no telling what fools will do when they get a little authority. Under the present Go- vernment it seems no honest man is safe in the Civil Service or in the Railway Depart- ment. Our readers will be pleased to hear that ex- Premier Reid is in excellent health. We have always recorded to George's credit that during his long administration the Kiama ghost had to hide its head.
