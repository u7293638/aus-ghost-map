A NEW INDUSTRY. A NEW calling 'has been brought into existence by ghosts and Mahatmas- chiefly the former-as the latter always fight amongst themselves, and initiate the public into their mysteries by family quarrels. With reference to the story of the haunted house at Peterborough, a gentleman who calls "himself "A Haunted House Expert," expresses his readiness to clear that particular building, or any other, of all the spirits which may be therein quicker than lightning. He sayshe has been honoured with the patronage of the nobility and gentry of various countries, and has been successfaul in extirpating some of the oldest ghosts in the kingdom. A "ghost-killer" must be quite a charming and pleasant occupation..
