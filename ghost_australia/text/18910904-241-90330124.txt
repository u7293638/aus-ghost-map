HOLY GHOST COLLEGE, BALLARAT. CONDUCTED BY THE FATHERS OF THE SOCIETY OF THE HOLY GHOST. This College is situated in the most healthy position of Ballarat, near Lake Wendouree, the Victoria Park, and the City Gardens. It is surrounded by spacious and beautiful grounds. The rooms and dor mitories are very lofty and well ventilated. The Curriculum includes preparation for the University and the various professions, as well as for the examinations required for public appointments. In the past year the pupils presented for matriculation and for public service were all successful. The general course comprises Ancient Classics, English, Mathematics, Natural Philosophy, and French. The latter language, as well as German, may be spoken daily with the French and German professors. \- Particular attention is devoted to the Modern or Commercial Course, intended for boys destined for mercantile pursuits. In addition to the subjects common to both the Classical and Commercial Courses, the latter comprises Book-Leeping, Commercial Arithmetic, Banking, Shorthand, Political Economy, and German. Within the last month important additions have been made to the staff.
