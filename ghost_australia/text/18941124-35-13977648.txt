ENTERTAINMENT AT THE RAILWAY INSTITUTE. Last evening the Newtown Electric Minstrels gave an entertainment in the Rail way Institute for the benefit of Mr Gus. Hooke, a railway employee, who met with a serious accident rendering him incapable of work. There was a large audience present The first part of the programme consisted of musical items, followed by step and clog dances, and concluded with a laughable farce entitled " The Ghost in the Pawn Shop " _
