A COLAC "GHOST." [BY TELEGRAPH.) MELBOURNE, Saturday. A "ghost" has been disturbing the in- habitants of Colac for some nights past. After several people and horses had been scared by the apparition - two or three ladies had fainted on seeing it-a series of investigations were carried out. As a result, the inquirers have discovered that a big tame bird has been made to fly about with a Chinese lantern tied to its tail.
