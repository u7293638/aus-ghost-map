THE UNIVERSITY OF MEL- BOURNE. At the meeting on Wednesday of the Trinity College Dialectic Society, there were present-the president (in the chair), the committee, about 25 members, and several visitors. The subject for debate was, " 'That this house believes in ghosts." A division was taken, with the following result: -Ayes, 11 ; noes, 15 , majority for noes, 4.
