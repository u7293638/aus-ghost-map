All over with John. Mr Quick M. L. A. has undertaken the Chinese Question. That settles it. Quick's mighty intellect has come out of its shell, where it has lain so long dormant, that its existence has been hitherto unsuspected, and the news will be flashed to China, that it is all over with the Mongolian. Not only will the Chinese have to clear, but anyone of the color of a Chinaman, is to be sacrificed. Three hundred millions of yellow-skins may now give up the ghost, and go out decently and commit suicide. The Quick will not have them-let them join the dead. ___
