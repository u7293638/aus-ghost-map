I ' SCHOOL OF ARTS. I To-night, at the School of Arts Professor Pepper will present an exceedingly good programme one which preserves the best features of those which preceded it, and supplements them by striking novelties The Portrait Limner will sketch a local celebrity, "Fisher's Ghost" will be repeated, the sculptures of ancient and modern times will be illustrated, the story of " say matron and Galatea" will be reproduced by the Metemsjchosii apparatus, and a spiritual seance, without spiritual aid will be given Microscopic wonders will be shown again, and an absurdity called 'No 27, 31, the missing ghost of the Red Lion Inn," will be produced.
