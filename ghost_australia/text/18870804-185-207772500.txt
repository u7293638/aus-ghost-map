INTERCOLONIAL NEWS. NEW SOUTH WALES. SYDNEY. Wednesday. [REUTER'S TELEGRAMS.] General Schaw is busily engaged on his in vestigation into the state of our defences. So far lie has been conducting his enquiries alone. The case against Murray, a Queensland digger, and Hannah Mullins, for having an illicit still in a house near the hay market, has been dismissed, there being no evidence that accused owned the still or occupied the house. The jury iu the inquest on the Penrith ghost case resumed sitting this morning, and further adjourned until Monday. The medi cal men still say that the bones fouud are human. A gold reef fouud three' miles from Cobar has caused great excitement iu the district, the land being secured by old miners all along the course of the reef. Charles, alias Captain Jenkin, a solicitor of the High Court of Chancery and an officer in the 19th Hussars, pleaded guilty to-day to obtaining goods by false pretences, and was sentenced to six months’ imprisonment. He was remanded an a charge of forging the name of a Victorian squatter. He has already served a sentence in Peutridge. The German squadron left the harbor to day, proceeding iu the first instance to Samoa. The railway authorities are considering the advisableuess of fitting goods trains with a continuous automatic brake. Somejpeculiur revelations were made before the Select Committee of the Legislative Council in regard to unlicensed medical prac titioners in the colony. One witness, who came from Melbourne some years ago, stated that he was now worth £100,000; he was originally an able seaman on board a war vesse.
