MOSMAN LITERARY AND DEBATING SOCIETY. The fortnightly meeting of the Mosman Literary and Debating Society in as held in the Council-chambers, Military-road, on Thursday night. Mr. Conroy was in the chair. Mr. L. Hopkins delivered a short lecture on Illustration in Journalism." Mr. W. M. Hamlet followed with a short lecture on "Minute Measurements of Length." Mr. Hinton read " A Modern Ghost Story, after which a recita- tion from Shakespeare was given. The evening's entertainment concluded with a trio rendered by Messrs. Jackschon, Hamlet, and M'Guire.
