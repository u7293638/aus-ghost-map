SALE OF RACEHORSES AT NEWMARKET. MELBOURNE, FRIDAY. A number of well-known racehorses were offered for sale | by auction to-day by Messrs. Yuille and Co., at their stables at Newmarket. There was a moderate attendance of buyers, and the bidding in some instances was brisk. The following prices were obtained Empress. 90 guineas i The Ghost, 55 guineas : Grace Darling, 250 guineas ; Kitty Fisher, ISO guineas; Lithologist, 55 guineas; Marl borough, 240 guineas The Knave, 140 guineas; Bothwell, 250 guineas.
