THE CYCLORAMA. Napoleon's ghost at the Cyolorama seems to have the same amount of fascination for the public as the ehost of Hamlet had for the young Dane. The attendance at the Cyolorama on Saturday evening was again large, and the illusion proved a complete success. Not only is the likeness to the original a perfect one, but the details in drees, even to the ooloun, are faithfully shown.
