A Substantial Ghost. o- - Some years ago a travelling troupo were playing Hamlet in Dublin. When the aotor representing the ghost came to the well known passage, " I am thy father's spirit," a voice from the gallery responded. " Ah, shure, an' a mighty fine spirit ye" make too." To which the ghost replied: " All right, Hagarty. I know yez, and I'll give yez such a batin' as yez never had in yure born days when I get yet outside." It is needless to say this brought down the house.
