DIOCESE OF KILDARE AND LEIGHLIN. VOTING FOR COADJUTOR-HISHOP. A solemn High Mass of the Holy Ghost was celebrated in the Cathedral, Carlow, on December 18, preparatory to the parish priests of the diocese office of Coadjutor to the venerable Lord Bishop of Kildare and Leighlin, and in succession to the lamented Most Rev. Dr. Comerford. The result was as follows :— The Very Rev. Patrick Foley, President Carlow College, and the Very Rev. Dr. Michael J. Murphy, P.P., V.G., Kildare, ex mquo dignissimi. — 14 votes each ; the Very Rev. Andrew Phelan, P.P., V.F., Maryborough, dignior—12 votes.
