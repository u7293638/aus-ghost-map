In the meanwhile many, thrown out of harness by the depression of the day, seize time by the forelock and flock to the scene Though a rush, in its main feature of attracting a crowd from all points of the compass to a common centre, has a close family resemblance to its fellow-rush, the rush of to-day widely varies in its component parts from the rushes of 40 years ago Then youth, energy, ability of brain or muscle, or both, of all countries and degrees, embarked heed. lessly upon a career and in a country of which they had no poor conception, and dug and delved in the most primitive manner with the rudest of appliances until the test or bitter experience had sorted those few for the toil of gold-digging from those whose bodily or mental equipment pointed out sure if less exciting methods of wooing fortune Now, all is different Every colony of the group has its array of miners with its attendant fringe, sometimes too heavy of mining speculators Some 400 of this admixture, with an additional 20 or 30 brave spirits of the fairer sex, are now crowding the ship from which I write as she seems sullenly to roll over the broad sea, and by the shining sand dunes of the coast, bound for Fremantle Of them individually little need be said. They are quiet and grave, of all ages and experiences, showing little excitement, though it is fair to suppose they feel it, and taking the whole thing as if it were a look into the columns of their morning paper to see if anything offered that might suit them.
