Lady doctors are fast making their presence felt in the medical profession, and, judging from the pass list for Bachelor of Surgery, just issued by the University of London, they are coming forward in ever-increasing numbers. Out of six candidates who have passed in the first division, three are ladies, all, it may be added, from the Royal Free Hospital. There were a score of passes in the second division, nine being successful lady candidates from the Royal Free Hospital, and the London School of Medicine.
