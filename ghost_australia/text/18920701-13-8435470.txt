A CRUEL FREAK AT ELSTERNWICK. A REWARD FOR THE DETECTION OF | THE PERPETRATOR. Considerable alarm has been experienced in the Elsternwick district for some time past by the unexpected appearance at night time in lonely parts of the district of a figure clad in white and with a strangely illumined face Several well authenticated cases in which persons have seen the perpetrator. of this mischievous and dangerous freak have been reported to the police Efforts have been made to capture the culprit, but have proved unavailing, as the person is astute enough to keep clear of any men and only appears before unaccompanied women or girls The subject w as referred to at We nesday's meeting of the Caulfield Council Councillor Sharp mentioned that a number of women had been seriously frightened in fact one lady had died partly owing to a fright His wife and a servant had also seen the ghost and the latter had become seriously ill in consequence It was eventually agreed to offer a reward of 100 for the arrest of the culprit
