OUR OWN SULLIVAN. SIR,—To the rescue-! The Royal would probably have enjoyed a larger audience last night, if -the other places of entertainment had been shut up. A perverse portion of the community went elsewhere; and that great artist Sullivan, who has come here all the way from Europe to civilise us, will consequently be the longer about making his pile. Is this to be tolerated ? Is transcendant merit to be dis- regarded ? Are the profane vulgar to spend their money where they please ? I pause for a reply.—Obediently yours, PEPPER'S GHOST.
