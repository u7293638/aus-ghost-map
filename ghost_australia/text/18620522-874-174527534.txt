Le Bonnet Rouge. The revolutionary effect of the Separation movement in the Western district was depicted in vivid colors at Belfast on Thursday last, the Gazette appearing on that day imprinted on red paper. The newsmongers omit to acquaint us whether the Editor and printers have committed themselves to political views of the same hue, or whether, like the Castlemaine ghost, they make their appearance sympathetically sans culottes. The Bard of the embryo Icolony is said to be hard at work in the elaboration of a Marseillaise, which is sure to raise the enthusiasm of the western colonist to concert pitch, and to force their Melbourne tyrants to draw in their horns.
