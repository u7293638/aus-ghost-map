NEW SOUTH WALES. SYDNEY, Wednesday A furious fight took place yesterday at Mudgee between John Patman and Lewis Long. The fight lasted some time, both men being severely punished before they were separated. During the night Long exhibited dangerous symptoms, and died before morning. Patman has been arrested.
