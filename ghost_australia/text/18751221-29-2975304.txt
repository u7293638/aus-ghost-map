WEATHERCOCKS. In the Legislative Hall, Once I heard the Fraser bawl, "All we want to make us free Is Responsibility." Then I heard a whisper say Increased office, increased pay,- ' Straight bold Fraser changed his tune : " No change jet, 'is much too soon." Handsome, sweet toned Hocking too ! Has this whisper reached to you ? May your leaves all turn to stone, And your wishes all prove bone. (Ghost of) F. P. B.
