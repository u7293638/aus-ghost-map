OPENING OF THE QUEENSLAND PARLIAMENT. [BY ELECTRIC TELEGRAPH.] GHOST OUR OWN CORRESPONDENT.) BRISBANE, Tuesday. Parliament was opened to-day with the usual ceremony The Governors speech states that the contract for direct mail communication will be submitted , also a bill to give authority to enter into a contract for the construction of lines to the Gulf of Carpentaria, in accordance with the proposals made to the Premier whilst in England Parliament will be invited to sanction proposals for augmenting the public income with the least possible inconvenience to the taxpayers Bills are promised to provide for the construction of local works on loan, management of waterworks, the regulation of Polynesian labour, the inspection of mines, the prevention of influx of criminals, and the issue of postal cards, c.
