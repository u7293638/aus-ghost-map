The annual retreat of the Roman Catho lic clergy of the archdiocese of Hobart has been held during this week, commencing on Monday, night and closing with the celebration of mass at 7 a.m. to-day. The exercises of the retreat were conducted by the Rev Father Hilary, of the Passionist Order. The annual synod was held in two ses sions, the first, a preparatory one, held on Thursday evening at the Archbishop's palace, and the second or solemn session in St. Mbary's Cathedral, commencing at eight o'clock. this morning.
