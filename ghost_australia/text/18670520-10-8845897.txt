LAW. POLICE COURT. SATURDAY, MAY 18TH, 1867. BEFORE A. B. Jones, Esq., Stipendiary Magistrate, PEACE DISTURBER Mary Gilbert fined 5s. for disturbing the peace. VAGRANTS. Thomas Ghost and William Brown sentenced to three months' hard labor for vagrancy in sleeping in an outhouse and not giving a good account of themselves. A case of maintenance was postponed till Wednesday. The Court then rose.
