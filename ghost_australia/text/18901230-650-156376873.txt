Serious Practical Joke. Sydney, Monday night. A serious accident the result of practical joking, has occurred at Wara station, near Wagga Wagga. A number of young men were spending the holidays at the station. One of them impersonated a ghost. Robing himself in a sheet he planted himself in a tree. Ho was seen and the joke was under stood. Getting a gun and drawing the shot, a youth fired at the ghost. , He had forgotten to draw the wad which penetrated the impersonator's chest, inflicting an ugly wound.
