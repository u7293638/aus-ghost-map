" It needs no Ghost to Tell us This." OK Monday last, THOMAS MORRISON was charged with a capital offence at the City Court. In his defence he stated he was a member of the New South Wales Assembly. To the latter assertion we confess doubts, for there is nothing in his appearance which could justify the supposition that he does not belong to the Lower House of Victorian Legislature.
