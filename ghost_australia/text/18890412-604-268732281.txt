QUITE USED TO BAD SPIRITS. A srpiiY ia told of a shrowish Scotchwoman who triod to wean her husband from tho drum* shop by employing hor brother to act tho part of n gliost, ana frighten John on his way homo, ‘Who arc you?’ asked tho guidiimn. ‘I’m Auld Nick/ wna tho reply. * Como awn’, moil/ said John, nothing daunted. Gio’s a shako o’ your hand; I nm married to a sister o’yours.’
