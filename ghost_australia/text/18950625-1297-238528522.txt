AN ISLAND TRADING VESSEL OVERDUE. News has been received from the Solo mon Islands that the inter-island trading vessel Sea Ghost, Captain M'Donald, was overdue at Rubiana, and fears wore entor- tained for her safety by some of the British traders in tho group. The Sea Ghost went out on a trading voyage to Augusta Bay, In Bonganvlllo Straits, and had not been heard of at Rubiana at last advices. She was then 13 days out. "The natives," writes a trader, "twice attempted to seize the ves sel some months back."
