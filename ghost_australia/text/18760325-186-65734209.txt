MARYBOROUGH. March 20. At the police-court examination of Rixon, brought up on the charge of murdering his wife, the prisoner declared that he saw the ghost of the murdered woman, who described the manner of her death ; that he then in- formed the police, who thereupon arrested him on the capital charge. March 22. The report of Professor Liversidge on the cause of rust in sugar cane says that rust is not a disease, but results from the absence of proper cultivation. He recommends that the megass be returned to the field immediately after it leaves the crushing rollers.
