Preserving Milk. Dr. von Baumhauer, Professor of Chem- istry to the Societe des Sciences in the Netherlands, has discovered a method by means of which he can preserve milk in its natural state without the addition of any foreign substance. Dr. von Baumhauer claims for his preparation that it is effected by purely physical means, and that when prepared it has shown itself practically capable of standing long voyages, and of retaining all its freshness for an indefinite period.
