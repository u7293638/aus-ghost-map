LifeatDunwich (Q), Benevolent Asylum cannot be one of unmixed joy, judging by the. manner in which two oE its oldest residents have shuffled off this mortal coil during the past three days. Jacob Hornung secreted a bread knife in his trousers on Tuesday morning last, and before tea was found with his jugular severed and his ' spirit departed.' On Thursday Michael Spollen, a decrepit old fellow of 89, by exercising a little strate gem managed to get into tho dispensary of the asylum, and when there drank a quantity of morphia, and shortly after wards gave' up the ghost.
