ALFRED MILLER AT KIAMA. the free-trade ghost. Kiama, This Bay. Mr. Alfred Miller delivered a lecture here on Monday evening on Free-trade. , The attendance was meagre. He Baid that, for the first time since he had been a Free-trade lecturer, he found himself in an unequivocally Free-trade electorate. The apathy of the people was a confirma tion of the fact. Where Protection abounded, enthusiasm on the part of Free-traders did more abound. Tho advocates of Protection aimed at pro ducing an artificial scarcity in tho interests of the producers of the artioles made scarce.
