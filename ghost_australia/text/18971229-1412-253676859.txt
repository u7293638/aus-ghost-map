ST. ANDREWS CHURCII. The Christmas service nt this church were splendidly attended 011 Christmas Day. Special Communion services wore held at 8 a.m. and 9 a.m., and morning prayer at II a.m. In the afternoon at 3 o'clock a children's festival service was held. At this service a number of carols were sung by the Sunday School scholars. A sormon appropriate to the season was preached by the Rev. A. Burton. The church was tastefully adorned with palms, Christmas tree blooms, and an abundance of garden flowers and green foliage. The decoration of the church was carried out by Mrs. Daw, Mrs. Peverill, and Misses Provis, Wyatt, and Daw (2). On Sunday there was a Communion service at 11 a.m. and a special festival at 2.39. With the usual evening meeting at 7.30 p.m. Special hymns were rendered at all services by the choir.
