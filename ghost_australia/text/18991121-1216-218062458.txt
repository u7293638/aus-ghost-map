SPOOKS ONCE MORE. According to a report from Blayney, some consternation has been caused among the miners at the copper mine there by the visitations of a ghost to one of the shafts. The men complain that they hear strange noises, bells ringing, sighings and groanings, and miners going below have their candles ex tinguished by some supernatural visitor. Some sbf men have left the mine owing to the terror the ghost has inspired.
