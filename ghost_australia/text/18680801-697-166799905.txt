BCAVEX. Monday. The Boomerang (s.) left for Sydney at noon, with 1800 oz. of gold on board. New diggings have been discovered, distant one hundred miles from here. The two gentlemen sent by the provincial committee to inspect them have re- turrod, and report favourably; they brought back five specimens. The committee are expected to send prospectors. Count Von Attem's yacht, ."' Hamlet's Ghost,"' arrived on a cruise.
