QUITE USED TO BAD SPIRITS. A stoey is told of a shrewish Scotchwoman who teed to wean her husband from the dram shop by employing her brother to act the part oi n ghost, aud frighten J ohn on his way home. 'Who are you?' asked the guidman. 'I'm tho reply. < Oome awa', mon,' said John, nothing daunted. Gie's a shake o' your hand ; I am married to a sister o' yours.'
