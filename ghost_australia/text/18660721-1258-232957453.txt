THE PHANTOM SHIP. A recent ' Extraordinary' contains the following startling announcement:— " The Roderick Dhu, schooner, of Richmond, was totally wrecked 14 miles from Port Stevens, Coonamlara, and arrived safe at Broken Bay." We imagine that even "Clan Alpin's warriors true " would be inclined to marvel at the appearance of their Chief under such circumstances as are related above. There must be a mistake somewhere. It was no doubt the " Rhoderick Dhu's " ghost which arrived at Broken Bay. Mr. Allen could, doubtless, throw some light upon the subject—He is an authority on ghost stories.
