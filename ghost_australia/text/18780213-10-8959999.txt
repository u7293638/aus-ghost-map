CORRECTIONS. TO THR KLUTOK OF THE MERCURY. SIR,—In my letter which you have done me the favour to publish in this day's issue of The Mercury, there occurs a quotation from memory, which, although fully and accurately embodying the substance, requires correction as to the precise wording of the passage, as follows :—"John xiv., 26. —But the Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, He shall teach you all things. " STEPHEN OWEN. Hobart Town, February 12.
