A SUBTERRANEAN GHOST. According to a report from Blayney some consternation has been caused amongst the miners employed at a copper mine by the visitation of a ghost in one of the shafts. The men say they hear strange noises, bells ringing, sighing., groanings, &c.; The miners declare they have had their candles extinguished by a supernatural visitor, and some allege that on different occasions something has brush ed ast them. Six men have left the mine owing to the terror the ghost has in- spired.
