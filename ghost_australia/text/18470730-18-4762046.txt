Queen's Theatre Royal. The Nights of Performance for the present will be Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. THIS EVENING (Friday), July 30th the performances will commence with a new farce entitled OUR MARY ANNE. Dance, Mr Harris. Song, Mr. Clarke: To be followed by MY HUSBAND'S GHOST: Song, Mr. Minor. The whole to conclude with an interest- ing drama, entitled THE EMIGRANT'S DAUGHTER. J. T. SMITH, Proprietor
