VICTORIAN AMATEUR TURF CLUB MEETING. [By Telegraph.] Melbourne, April 23. The following horses have been left in the Victorian Amateur Turf Club races, to be held at Caulfield on Saturday:—Glen Huntly Handi cap — Lord Wilton, Bohemian, Yellow Hammer, Duration, Uncas, East Lynne Muscat, Surprise, Grace Darling, Lucky Hit Ghost, Velocipede. Steeplechase—Topsail Beresford, Pride of the Hills, Native, Black thorn, Kingston, Dollar, Deceiver, Tansticker, Chester, Sir Samuel, Granville, Clarence, Royal Maid.
