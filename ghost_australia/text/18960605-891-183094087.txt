Cemetery Ghost Fined. Adelaide. Mav 29. At the Police Court yesterday a man namesd A Rudd was charged with unlawfully dis- guising himself with a piece of white and, therefore, to be deemed idle and dis- orderly. On the night of May G, Thomas Medal was on his way home when -he noticed a figure in white emerge from the Wealeyan cemetery. As it approached him he heard it say, "Thomas Medal, I claim you!" He answered, "Take me, then, and struck it with a stick which he carried. The ghost closed with him, and in the scuffle the sheet was dis- arranged and Medal identified Rudd.
