FOOTBALL RECORD. TO THE EDITOR. —yours, &C;, CALLEGIAN, 30th September SIR, —Kindly allow me to contradict a statement which appeared in yesterday's issue in reference to the schools' premiership The correct record is as follows:—Ballarat College—Played 8, non 6, lost 1, drawn 1, points, 26 Holy Ghost—Played 7, won 5, lost 1, drawn 1; points, 22 The HG C correspondent states that they hate played 8 matches; whereas they have yet to meet the Creswick Grammar school in a premier ship contest Thanking you in anticipation.
