AN ALLEGED GHOST. A good deal of excitement has been created in the Morpeth district by the appearance a few nights ago of a ghostly object on the back road from East Maitland to Morpeth. A number of the residents have decided to elucidate the mystery, and there is ever pro- bability that during the evenings for some little time the thoroughfare will be watched. The specific gravity of any supernatural looking object will, it is understood, be dis- covered by what is known as the "rock salt " process. (For continuation of Maitland News see Page 7)
