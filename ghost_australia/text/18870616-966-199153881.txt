THE CBADOCK " GHOST." [By Telegraph.] Hammond, June 15. A number of people assembled here to-day to hear the informations against tho Hamdorf family, but the cases were not brought on, M.C. Thomas stating that he had been directed to await further instructions before proceeding with them. Therefore the summonses had not been served. The Justices present adjourned the Court until July 7. Several who had witnessed the seances were present, and various opinions regarding the knocking were expressed. It is understood that subscriptions are being raised to provide counsel for the defence.
