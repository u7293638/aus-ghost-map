Accident in FSarfiiet-street. A rather serious accident happened in Market street at about 3 o'clock Saturday afternoon. An old woman named Elizabeth Ghost was crossing the road near Her Majesty's Theatre, when she was knocked down by a buggy. The woman was lame, and it is alleged that she went right in front of the bugey. She was taken to the Sydney Hospital, where it was found that she had received a wound over the left eye. She was admitted for treatment.
