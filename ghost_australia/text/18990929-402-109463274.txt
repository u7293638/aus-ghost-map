They Came Only with the Conqueror. The ghost of William of Normandy heaved a heavy sigh. " I've been dabbling a bit in statistic lately," it said to the phantom of good King John, " and I find, to my chagrin, I that I can't personally claim an atom of praise for having won the battle of Hastings." " Good heavens, Billy ! how's that ?" " Why, judging from the number of different families who are boasting that their ancestors came over with the Con- queror, I calculate that I must have in- vaded England with an army of about twelve hundred and fifty thousand strong."
