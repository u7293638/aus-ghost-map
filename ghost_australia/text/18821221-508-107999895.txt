Newcastle Items. Newcastle, Thursday. The Newcastle Fire Brigade and Honeysuckle Volunteer Fire Brigade had a supper at the Ter minus Hotel last night. About 70 were present, .and great cordiality was displayed among the members. A rumour having got about that a ghost had been seen on the rocks, near the wreck of the City : of Newcastle during Wednesday night, a large party of Glebe residents assembled at 1 o'clock this morning and marched over the sand-drift to the spot, but the apparition was not forthcoming.
