" Can you tell me where Mr. Hunter lives ?" said the statesman. " It's no use your going there," was the unexpected reply. " But do you know wkere he lives ?" "Faith an' I do; but it's no use going there." The inquirer began to get angry. "I didn't ask your advice. I simply .want to know where Mr. Hunter lives." " Oh, well, he lives down the street yonder, the first house round the corner; but I toll you it's no use your going there, for I've just been there myself, and he's al ready got a man."
