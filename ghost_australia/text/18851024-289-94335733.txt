CAULFIELD TRAINING NOTES. [By Telegraph. 1 Melbourne, October 22. Kingship made his first appearance since the accident at the Caulfield Cup meeting. He did slow work. Louisa Berta and Here Goes ran home from the three- furlong post. Blink Bonny and Hippogriff cantered and spurted. Lando did a strong mile, and Grace Darling, Nuncio, and Ghost did mild exercise.
