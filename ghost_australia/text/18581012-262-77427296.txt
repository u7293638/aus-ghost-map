Thursday, October 7. Before the Police Magistrate. Thomas Fallon, on ticket-of-leave from Cockatoo, was charged with loitering about, without means of subsistence. The chief constable proved that he had been offered work and refused. For his defence prisoner stated that he was going to his employment across the Gundagai River, but he was prevented by Stanley's ghost, and was knocked about from pillar to post. The bench sentenced him to one month's imprisonment in Brisbane gaol, and directed that the sheriff be informed of the state of the pri- soner's mind.
