Spiritualism. Mr. John Scouller has written a pamphlet on "Spiritualism, What and Whence it is." He says it is " an independent investigation and exposition by a business man." These last words excite suspicion. How comes " a business man" to Know anything about spiritualism? Hud the author been a doctor, a lawyer, a parson, or even a poet, this men* lenged. Mr. Scouller writes like a man who 13 thoroughly sincere; and he says that the con clusions to which he has arrived are wholly his own. Anyone desiring to know what Mr. Scouller. has to say must read his pamphlet. But there is one thing which he has not ex plained, that is, where the ghosts get their clothes from. The book is published by A. H. Massing and Co., Melbourne.
