TUMUT. TUESDAY. The police last night arrested a man on suspicion of being Joseph Byrnes the bushranger. They have watched him for a long time. Collins, the Tumut blacksmith, know Byrnes when he was a groom in Victoria, and said that he brought horses frequently to him for shoeing. Collins pointed Byrnes out amid a group in the Royal Hotel billiard-room, and he says if the man is not Byrnes it must be his ghost. Prisoner was remanded for further identification.
