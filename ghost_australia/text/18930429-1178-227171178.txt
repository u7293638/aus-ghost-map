FIVE DOCK. The usual meeting of this council was held on Thursday night, Alderman Croker presiding. Aldermen M'Fadyen, Myler, West and the council clerk (Mr. H. Fraser) were also present. Of the fow letters placed before tho meet ing tho most important was that coming from Alderman Ghost, who wrote tendering his resignation, owing to ths firm of which he was a member having sequestrated its estate. A letter from the Excelsior Association directed' attention to a nuisance arising from skins and offal kept on the premises of a North Fivo Dock resident.
