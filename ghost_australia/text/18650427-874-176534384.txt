Corroborative Opinion. THE Perth Inquirer (Western Australia) says, we are "asturdy knot of pseudo- pietists and knares, who have humbugged the Ministry into the belief that the purity of Eastern Australia was clouded by the presence of the convict establishment in Western Australia." Messrs. GILBERT, HALL, and DUNN present their compliments to the editor of the Perth Inquirer, and beg to say that they entirely coincide in his views. MORGAN'S ghost has sent a similar message.
