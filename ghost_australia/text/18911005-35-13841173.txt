The Sydney Flying Squadron Yacht Club. A race in connection with the above club will be sailed this afternoon over a triangular course. The boats entered with their handicaps are as follows : Kannanook, Regina, and Lottie, scratch ; Our Tom, 2 minutes ; Ghost, 3 minutes ; and Violet, 3½ minutes. The prizes are £5, £2, and £1, being first, second, and third respectively.
