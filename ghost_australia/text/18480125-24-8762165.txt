IMPROMPTU. In truth, O Justice! thou art very just In weighing out thy wares--sold at per pound! Justice for gold--not justice upon trust. Justice to give the poor would be unjust! Giving with selling justice you confound. Justice, you know, is represented blind, Justice, I know, is deaf as any post! Justice, if just, is also counted kind; "Colonial justice" is not to my mind, Of Justice nothing but the ghastly ghost! JUSTICE is sold and meted to the rich; JUSTICE but mocks and ridicules the poor! JUSTICE, I know, is a most perjured b---- JUSTICE should have just trial as a witch, JUSTICE is sick--and hopeless is her cure.
