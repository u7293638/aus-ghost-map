PLAYING THIE GHOST. BY THREE YOUTHS. HOBART, Friday. At the Police Court to- day three youths, named Joseph Archer, Albert and James Fulton, were sent to gaol for 14 days for playing the ghost at Bellerive on Tuesday last. Mr. Ber hard Shaw, Police Magistrate, in pas sing sentence, described the act as most atrocious, and it was likely to cause in calculable injury to timid persons.
