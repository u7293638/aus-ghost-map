Thrashing a ' Ghost.' At West Stafford, a village near Dorchester (E.), considerable excite ment had been caused by the rumors of ghosts waltzing nightly. Ulti mately a search party was formed, both, men and women, being armed with sticks. On arriving at a gate they saw a figure upon it draped in white ; the crowd pressed forward, and the leaders severely thrashed the ghost, who screamed for mercy.
