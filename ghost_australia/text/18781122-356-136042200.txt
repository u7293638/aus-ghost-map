WEST MZAITLAND Bussi FIRE.-A large bush fire was raging in the vicinity of Dagworth, a place about five miles from Maitland, all day yesterday. This fire extended fora considerable distance, and dense volumns of smoke were wafted over the town, a strong westerly was blow ing, and was disagreeably hot; no doubt the extent of the fire had a great deal to do with this.
