THE UNIVERSITY OF MEL BOURNE. "MATRICULATION EXAMINATION, DECEMBER, 1889. Appended is the alphabetical list of the successful candidates for the matriculation examination, as posted on the University notice board yesterday. The list also shows the last place at which each of the candidates received instruction. The table usually published in connection with this examination will appear in Monday's issue givingin each subject the total number of candidates presented, the total who passed, the total who failed. the total who obtained honours and the percentage of failures.
