NEW SOUTH WALES. {"From our own Correspondent.] Sydney, March 9. Aq anti-Redmond meeting was held at Newcastle last night at the Victoria Theatre, which "was crammed to the doors. Enthu siastic cheers were given for the Queen, and the lecturer, the Rev. John Osborne, of New castle. A so-called ghost at Shepherd's Hill, New castle, attracts large numbers nightly. The peculiar phenomenon seems to be the result of phosphorescence, generally visible from 9 to midnight, when the tide is not too high.
