VICTORIAN SPORTING NEWS. [By Telegraph. 1 Melbourne, August 9. The only betting on Saturday was for the doubles the Caumeld Hurdle and Steeplechase, Huntsman and Vulcan being backed at 200 to 7, the same being taken about Huntsman and Mernder, Star aud Wymlet, and for Ghost and Wymlet 1,000 to 3 was taken.
