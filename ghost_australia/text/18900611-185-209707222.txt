FOOTBALL. Central play Buninyong on Saturday, at Buninyong. Train leaves West station at 2.40 p.m. The first contest of the football season be- tween teams representing the Holy Ghost College and Ballarat College will take place to-day, on the Saxon paddock. Play to begin at 3 p.m. sharp. The following will represent the B.G.C.:—Casey, Donaghy, Dowling, Field, Green, Hill, Hurley, Kin- sella, Maloney, Mooney, M‘Carthy, M’Lean, M’Manamny (2), Noonan, O'Brien, Sheehan (2), Stratton, Smithwick.
