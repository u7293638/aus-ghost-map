FOOTBALL. The second and final match between the Ballarat College and Holy Ghost College took place on the Saxon paddock yesterday. The game throughout was very exciting, and resulted in a well-earned victory for the Ballarat College by two goals five behinds to one goal one behind. The -winners played a spirited game, and gained the victory over their weightier opponents only by good, con certed play. Those who figured prominently among them were Martin, Esplin, Smith (cipt.), Lees, M'Clelland, and Wanliss.
