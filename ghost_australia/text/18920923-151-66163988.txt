MUNIOIPAL NOTES. Animate3 discussion at the Flemington Council last Tuesday. The returning officers' election expenses account unanimously passed after all the unnecessary bother. Cr. Davis very active in proposing new works, Cr. Pridham thinks that a 5e. rate won't cover the cost of all the works proposed by Or. Davis. The Flemington Bridge to be widened at last, thanks to the "new blood." Cr. Barrett thinke that annexation won't take place yet awhile. New court-house at Flemington to be opened next Tuesday, Lawn tennis court ready also. The Flemington bowling green looking very well-ready for play in November, The Flemington ghost still walking.
