WARRNAMBOOL. The youth who circulated a story about having encountered a ghost-like figure near the cemetery lately a report which scared nervous residents a good deal has confessed to the police under examination that the story was without foundation. An illustration of the rate at which Lady Hay is becoming silted up is afforded by a full bottle of colonial beer being brought up in the dredge. It was not recovered in the first scoop, but in the second, and therefore must have been covered by about. 3ft. of sand. The recent floods in the Merri River have had a marked effect on the sand deposits in the bay, a further considerable encroachment being noted. The number of persons entitled to vote in the Warrnambool electorate a year ago was 2,000, and this is expected to be in creased by fully 30)0.
