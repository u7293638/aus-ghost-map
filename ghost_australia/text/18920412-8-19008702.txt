Hot His Kind. A German one day expressed himself as being somewhat offended-because an American gentle- man had asserted that his Teutonic countrymen could not, as a rule, appreciate American jokes. " Try one on me !" said he defiantly, and the American accordingly told him the story of the tree " out West " which was BO high that it took two men to see to the top. One of them saw as far as he could, and then the second began to look at the spot where the first stopped seeing. The recital did not raise the ghost of a smile upon the German's face, and the other said to him ! "Well, you see the joke is lost on you. You' can't appreciate American humour." " Oh, but," said the German, " dat's not humour. Dat's von lie !"
