Several persons, the miserable man's little boy, ten years old amongst the rest, swore to those articles as having been the property of Jane Jones and worn by her when last seen. The boy's evi- dence is given at great length and is extremely interesting. Upon this and other evidence, the chain of which is complete, the jury re- turned a verdict that the body was the remains of Jane Jones, who had been wilfully murdered by Daniel Good.
