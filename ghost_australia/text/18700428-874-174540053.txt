Nautical Intelligence' THE crew of the British Columbia are hailing the skipper of the Spreadeagle Columbia, asking to be taken in tow, as they are short-handed, and otherwise generally disabled. They are also dissatisfied with their present commander, one JOHN BULL, because he cannot alter their geographical position, and make the clouds drop immigrants and dollars as well as fatness. On the other hand, they say that " With Uncle SAM at the helm, all those luxuries would be attainable without requiring anything in the way of exertion on their part."
