YOUNG WOMEN'S CHRISTIAN ASSOCIATION, SURRY HILLS BRANCH. A special meeting of the above was held in St. David's Hall on Monday evening to hear an address by the incident, Mrs. Ward, on " The Baptism of the Holy Ghost, What is it, and how is it to be ob tainted *>" There it is a large attendance of members. On the same evening a meeting of the committee was hold when the sector fry, Miss Johnson, reported a large increase of members, and a good average attendance. Miss Smith, hon. treasurer, reported that the furnaces were in a satisfactory condition.
