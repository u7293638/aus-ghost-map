SCHOOL OF ARTS. The School of Arts was crowded in every- portion last night. The entertainment commenced with more effects and new wonders from the "Modern Metempsychosis," followed by the " Popular Portrait Limmer," and alter timE Professor Pepper delivered a very interesting lecture entitled "A walk through London," which he illustrated by some really beautiful views of the city and its most recent improvements. "Fisher's Ghost," the photographs of ancient and modern statuary, the spiritual seance, and the farce " The missing Ghosts" were also included in this very attractive programme. To-night Professor Pepper will make his last appearance in this hall. In addition to many other interesting features Professor Pepper will burn a diamond, and afterwards analyse its contents, and illustrate a walk through the Paris Exposition.
