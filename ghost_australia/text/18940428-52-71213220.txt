Amy Iiobsart's Ghost. SALE OE CUMNOR PLACE. . LONDON, April 17.-Mr. Hall, a gentle- man who ' purchased Cumnor Place from the Earl of Abingdon, believing it to be the Cumnor Place celebrated for the murder of Amy Robsart, and reported to be haunted by her ghost, is now suing for a rescinding of the purchase, owing to the unhistoric nature of the Place's claim to be considered the scene of the occurrence. The trial concluded to-day. The jury returned a verdict for the Earl of Abingdon.
