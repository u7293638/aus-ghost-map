According to private cablegrams received in London it is asserted that shots have been exchanged between the Chinese and Japanese warships in Corean waters. It is also said that the Japanese gun- boats are bombarding the coast of Corea. The Earl of Kimberley, Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, is earnestly urging the two nations to maintain peace.
